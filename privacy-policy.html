Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: November 2024

Fry's Excavating & Grading ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and project location when you fill out our contact form or call us.
  • Project Details: Information about your excavating, grading, demolition, hauling, or land clearing needs that you provide to us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent on the site (if analytics are enabled).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes for services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Schedule and complete excavating, grading, and related services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business (e.g., email services).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps: We embed Google Maps to show our service area. Google may collect data according to their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect data as described in their privacy policy.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
