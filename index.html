FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common things customers ask us.

How much does excavating or grading cost?

Every project is different, so costs vary based on the scope of work, site conditions, and equipment needed. We provide free, no-obligation estimates so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We offer free estimates for all our services. Just give us a call or fill out the contact form, and we'll schedule a time to assess your project and provide a clear, straightforward quote.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Godfrey, IL and serve the surrounding communities including Alton, Bethalto, Brighton, Jerseyville, Edwardsville, Glen Carbon, Wood River, Grafton, and more. If you're unsure, just ask!

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully insured. We carry liability insurance to protect your property and our crew. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines depend on the size and complexity of the job. Small residential projects might take a day or two, while larger jobs could take a week or more. We'll give you a time estimate along with your quote.

What should I do to prepare for excavation work?

Make sure the work area is accessible and clear of vehicles or obstacles. If you have underground utilities, let us know so we can coordinate locates. We'll walk you through everything during the estimate.
