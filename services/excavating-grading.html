Excavating & Grading Services

Precision earthwork to prepare your property for construction, drainage solutions, and more.

Professional Excavating & Grading in Godfrey, IL

Whether you're building a new home, adding an outbuilding, or need to correct drainage issues on your property, Fry's Excavating & Grading has the equipment and expertise to get the job done right. We handle projects of all sizes, from small residential jobs to larger commercial site preparation.

Proper excavating and grading is the foundation of any successful construction project. Poorly prepared ground can lead to structural issues, water damage, and costly repairs down the road. Our team ensures your site is leveled correctly and graded for proper drainage from the start.

Why Proper Grading Matters

  • Prevents water damage – Correct slope directs water away from your foundation
  • Ensures structural stability – Level, compacted soil prevents settling and shifting
  • Improves drainage – Eliminates standing water and soggy areas in your yard
  • Prepares for construction – Creates the right base for concrete, pavers, or landscaping
  • Increases property value – Well-graded land is more attractive and functional

Our Process

Every project starts with a site visit to assess your needs and discuss your goals. We'll provide a clear, straightforward quote with no hidden fees. Once we begin work, we keep you informed throughout the process and leave your site clean when we're done.

  1. Site Assessment – We evaluate the terrain, soil conditions, and your project requirements
  2. Planning – We determine the best approach and provide a detailed estimate
  3. Excavation – We remove or relocate earth as needed using the right equipment for the job
