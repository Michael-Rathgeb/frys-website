Professional Demolition Services in Godfrey, IL

Need to clear an old structure from your property? Fry's Excavating & Grading provides safe, efficient demolition services for residential and commercial properties. From small outbuildings to full house demolitions, we have the equipment and experience to handle your project.

Our demolition services include complete tear-down, debris removal, and site cleanup. We work carefully to minimize impact on surrounding areas while getting the job done quickly and safely.

Safety First Approach

Demolition work requires careful planning and execution. Here's how we keep everyone safe:

  • Thorough site assessment before any work begins
  • Proper equipment and trained operators
  • Dust and debris control measures
  • Coordination with utility companies when needed
  • Full liability insurance coverage

Our Demolition Process

  1. Site Evaluation – We assess the structure, identify any hazards, and plan the safest approach
  2. Permits & Utilities – We help coordinate necessary permits and utility disconnections
  3. Demolition – Systematic tear-down using appropriate equipment for the job
  4. Debris Removal – All materials hauled away and disposed of properly
  5. Site Cleanup – Final grading and cleanup so your site is ready for what's next
